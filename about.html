OUR STORY

Founded in 1993, CGTI started out providing GPS survey services and training other professionals in GPS technology. CGTI serviced the mapping industry and carried out GPS campaigns for first and second order regional control surveys across North America, Latin America, and the Caribbean. It then morphed into a high precision surveys service for alignments and deformation monitoring. This was applied to tunnelling surveys.

From 2019, CGTI narrowed its focus to the high precision surveys required in Tunnelling, Alignments, Monitoring and deformation applications. Today the focus is providing a total solution to your tunnelling project survey needs.

Mission

We are a North American company training and supporting tunnel surveying Engineers to provide all things in tunnel surveying to your tunneling projects in the Americas.

Vision

To be the most efficient and reliable tunnel measuring company supporting the tunneling industry.
