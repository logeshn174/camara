SURVEYING AND MAPPING

Camara Geomatic Technologies Inc (CGTI) is an innovative professional surveying firm focused on providing engineering surveying services to the tunnelling industry in Canada and USA. We bring a great deal of experience in precise surveying of large infrastructure projects, and a deep understanding of surveying & mapping and the needs of all the other disciplines in large engineering projects. Our experience and expert knowledge of instruments and measuring techniques, adjustment and analysis is brought to bear on every task.
